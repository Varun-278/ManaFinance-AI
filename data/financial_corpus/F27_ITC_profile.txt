Company: ITC Ltd.
Industry: Consumer Goods / Tobacco / Hotels / Paper
ISIN: INE154A01025
Symbol: ITC

1. Business Overview
ITC is a diversified conglomerate with core businesses in cigarettes, FMCG foods and personal care, hotels, paperboards & packaging, and agri-business.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Cigarette sales (high-margin, core profit engine).
- Branded packaged foods (Aashirvaad, Sunfeast, Bingo).
- Personal care and hygiene products.
- Hotels, paper & packaging, and agri-trading.

3. Cost Structure
- Tobacco and agri raw materials.
- Excise and taxes on cigarettes (very high).
- Advertising and branding for FMCG.
- Hotel operating costs.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Dominant market share in cigarettes.
- Strong backward linkage in agri and paper.
- Fast-growing FMCG portfolio riding brand equity.
- Very high ROCE from cigarettes funding diversification.

5. Risks
- Regulatory and tax risks on tobacco.
- Slow ramp-up in hotel profitability.
- Competition in FMCG from HUL, Nestlé, etc.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Historically seen as a “value + dividend” stock.
- Low-to-moderate volatility.
- Volume surges during tax policy changes and demerger/structure news.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
ITC is:
- A cash cow (cigarettes) funding optionality (FMCG hotels, paper),
- Low beta with attractive dividend yield.

Sum-of-the-parts valuation often applied:
- High PE for FMCG,
- Lower PE for cigarettes.

8. Outlook
- Continued FMCG premiumisation.
- Structural headwinds in tobacco balanced by brand strength.
