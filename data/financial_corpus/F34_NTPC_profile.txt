Company: NTPC Ltd.
Industry: Energy (Power Generation)
ISIN: INE733E01010
Symbol: NTPC

1. Business Overview
NTPC is India’s largest power generation company operating thermal, hydro, solar, and wind plants. It plays a critical role in India’s base-load electricity supply.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Regulated tariff-based thermal generation.
- Renewable energy capacity additions.
- Coal-based generation under long-term PPAs.

3. Cost Structure
- Coal procurement.
- Plant maintenance and operations.
- Depreciation on large plants.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Regulated business with assured returns.
- Largest power generation fleet in India.
- Strong execution pipeline in renewable capacity.

5. Risks
- Rising renewable penetration reducing thermal PLFs.
- Coal supply constraints.
- Environmental/regulatory compliance costs.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Stable, low-volatility PSU behavior.
- Volume changes during RE policy updates.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
NTPC acts like:
- A regulated utility with predictable ROE,
- Defensive, low-beta stock,
- Valuation driven by capacity addition and regulated equity returns.

8. Outlook
- Major renewable energy expansion roadmap.
- Green hydrogen and storage initiatives.
