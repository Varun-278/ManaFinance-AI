BANKING SYSTEM IN INDIA 

1. STRUCTURE
• Public sector banks
• Private sector banks
• Foreign banks
• Cooperative banks
• Small finance banks
• Payments banks

2. KEY PERFORMANCE METRICS
• NIM (Net Interest Margin)
• CASA ratio
• AUM growth
• GNPA / NNPA
• Credit cost
• Capital adequacy (CRAR)

3. REGULATIONS (RBI)
• Basel III norms
• Prompt Corrective Action (PCA)
• Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)
• Statutory guidelines (CRR, SLR)

4. NPA CYCLE DYNAMICS
NPA rises due to:
• Economic slowdown
• Sectoral stress (infra, power)
• Poor underwriting
• Corporate over-leveraging

Repair mechanisms:
• IBC (Insolvency code)
• ARC sales
• Write-offs + provisioning

5. BANK BUSINESS MODELS
Retail-focused banks (HDFC Bank, Kotak):
• Lower credit risk
• Higher fee income

Corporate-focused banks:
• Higher yields
• Higher NPA risk

6. DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION
• UPI dominance
• Digital KYC
• Neo-banking models
• Automation in credit scoring

7. RISKS
• ALM mismatch
• Wholesale funding dependence
• Credit concentration
• Interest rate cycles
