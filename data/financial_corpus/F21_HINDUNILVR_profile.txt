Company: Hindustan Unilever Ltd. (HUL)
Industry: Consumer Goods (FMCG)
ISIN: INE030A01027
Symbol: HINDUNILVR

1. Business Overview
HUL is India’s largest FMCG company with a diversified portfolio across home care, beauty & personal care, foods & refreshments. Brands include Surf Excel, Lux, Dove, Pepsodent, Clinic Plus, and Kwality Wall’s.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Everyday consumption categories (soap, detergents, hair care).
- Premiumisation in beauty and personal care.
- Penetration into rural markets.
- Ice cream and beverages portfolio.

3. Cost Structure
- Raw materials (palm oil, milk, packaging).
- Advertising and promotion.
- Distribution logistics.
- Royalty and brand fees to Unilever.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Massive distribution reach (7M+ outlets).
- Strong brand equity.
- Data-driven marketing and supply chain.
- Diversified category presence reduces cyclicality.

5. Risks
- High sensitivity to commodity cost spikes.
- Competition from Patanjali, ITC, and local brands.
- Royalty fee pressure impacting profitability.

6. Dataset-Based Behavior
- Very low volatility (defensive FMCG nature).
- Price stability with long upward drift.
- Volume spikes near results due to institutional flows.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
HUL is:
- A classic low-beta, cash-generating consumer staple,
- With high ROCE and strong pricing power.

Valuation driven by:
- Margin resilience,
- Volume growth,
- Brand strength.

8. Outlook
- Rural recovery and premium product growth.
- Expanding into nutrition and beauty tech.
