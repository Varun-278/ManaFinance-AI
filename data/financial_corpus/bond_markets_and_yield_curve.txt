BOND MARKETS & YIELD CURVE 

1. BOND MARKET STRUCTURE
Segments:
• Government securities (G-Secs)
• State development loans (SDLs)
• Corporate bonds
• Money market instruments (T-Bills, CP, CD)

2. YIELD CURVE
A plot of yields vs maturities.
Shapes:
• Upward sloping (normal)
• Flat (transition)
• Inverted (signals slowdown)

3. DRIVERS OF YIELD MOVEMENTS
• Inflation expectations
• RBI policy stance
• Liquidity conditions
• Fiscal deficit & borrowing program
• Global bond yields

4. SPREAD ANALYSIS
Corporate bond spreads measure:
• Credit risk
• Liquidity premium
• Market stress

5. KEY BOND CONCEPTS
Duration:
Measures price sensitivity to yield changes.

Convexity:
Measures curvature of price–yield relationship.

Term Premium:
Extra yield for holding long-term bonds.

6. MARKET PARTICIPANTS
• Mutual funds
• Banks
• Insurance companies
• Pension funds
• FPIs

7. RISKS
• Interest rate risk
• Credit risk
• Liquidity risk
• Reinvestment risk
