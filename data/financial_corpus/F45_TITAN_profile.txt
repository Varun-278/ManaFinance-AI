Company: Titan Company Ltd.
Industry: Consumer Goods (Jewelry, Watches, Eyewear)
ISIN: INE280A01028
Symbol: TITAN

1. Business Overview
Titan is India’s largest organized jewelry retailer (Tanishq), also operating in watches (Titan), eyewear, and accessories.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Gold jewelry (major share).
- Eyewear expansion.
- Premium watches and wearables.

3. Cost Structure
- Gold procurement (hedged).
- Retail store expansion.
- Marketing and branding.

4. Competitive Strengths
- Strong brand trust in jewelry.
- Shift from unorganized to organized retail.
- Pan-India retail presence.

5. Risks
- Gold price volatility.
- Festive-season dependence.
- Competition from regional jewelers.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Low–moderate volatility.
- Strong uptrend due to structural consumption shift.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
A premium consumer compounder with:
- High ROCE,
- Strong cash flows,
- High valuation multiples justified by growth visibility.

8. Outlook
- Accelerated Tanishq expansion,
- Growth in premium categories and eyewear.
