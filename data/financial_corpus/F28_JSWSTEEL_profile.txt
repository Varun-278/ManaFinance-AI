Company: JSW Steel Ltd.
Industry: Metals (Steel)
ISIN: INE019A01038
Symbol: JSWSTEEL

1. Business Overview
JSW Steel is one of India’s largest integrated steel producers with operations spanning flat and long products, coated steel, and value-added steel for automotive, infrastructure, and construction.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Domestic and export steel volumes.
- Realisations linked to global steel prices.
- Value-added segments (auto, coated, specialty steels).

3. Cost Structure
- Iron ore, coking coal, and power costs.
- Logistics and freight.
- Capex for capacity expansion and modernization.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Efficient, modern plants with high capacity utilisation.
- Strong presence in value-added steel.
- Vertical integration in raw materials (iron ore mines).

5. Risks
- High sensitivity to global steel cycles.
- Raw material price spikes compressing spreads.
- Trade barriers (duties, quotas).

6. Dataset Behavior
- High volatility compared to defensives.
- Large volume swings around global steel price movements and capex announcements.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
JSW behaves like a classic cyclical:
- Earnings expand sharply in upcycles,
- Contract in downcycles.

Valuation hinges on:
- EBITDA/ton,
- Leverage levels,
- Capex discipline.

8. Outlook
- Capacity expansion to capture infrastructure-led demand.
- Focus on value-added and specialty steels for autos, renewables.
