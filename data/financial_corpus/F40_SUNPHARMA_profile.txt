Company: Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd.
Industry: Pharmaceuticals
ISIN: INE044A01036
Symbol: SUNPHARMA

1. Business Overview
Sun Pharma is India’s largest pharma company with strengths in generics, specialty products (especially dermatology), and branded prescriptions in India.

2. Revenue Drivers
- US dermatology & generics.
- India branded formulations.
- Specialty pharma (Ilumya).
- Emerging markets.

3. Cost Structure
- R&D expenditure,
- Manufacturing costs,
- Compliance and regulatory adherence.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Leadership in chronic therapies in India.
- Specialty pharma pipeline improving margins.
- Strong distribution and doctor-connect ecosystem.

5. Risks
- US FDA plant compliance issues.
- US generics pricing pressure.
- Currency fluctuations.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Moderate volatility with defensive tendencies.
- Volume surges around specialty drug approvals.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
Sun behaves like:
- A hybrid of generic + specialty pharma,
- Margin expansion story driven by specialty launches.

8. Outlook
- Higher specialty contribution,
- Expanding dermatology footprint.
