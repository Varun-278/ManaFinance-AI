COMMODITY MARKETS 

1. MARKET SEGMENTS
• Energy (crude oil, natural gas)
• Metals (gold, silver, copper, aluminum)
• Agricultural commodities

2. DRIVERS OF COMMODITY PRICES
• Global supply-demand imbalance
• Geopolitical tensions
• Weather shocks (agri)
• US Dollar strength
• OPEC decisions (oil)

3. COMMODITIES & INFLATION
• Input costs influence CPI & WPI
• Oil shocks → cost-push inflation

4. GOLD MARKET DYNAMICS
Gold influenced by:
• USD movements
• Real interest rates
• Risk sentiment
• Central bank purchases

5. INDUSTRIAL METALS
Linked to:
• Global manufacturing cycle
• China demand
• Infrastructure spending

6. ENERGY MARKETS
Crude oil key factors:
