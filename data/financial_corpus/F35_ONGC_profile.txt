Company: Oil & Natural Gas Corporation Ltd.
Industry: Energy (Upstream Oil & Gas Exploration)
ISIN: INE213A01029
Symbol: ONGC

1. Business Overview
ONGC is India's largest oil & gas explorer engaged in offshore/onshore exploration, production, and gas development. It supplies crude to domestic refiners and holds major stakes in overseas assets via OVL.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Crude oil production volumes.
- Realizations linked to global Brent prices.
- Natural gas pricing.
- Petroleum subsidy sharing (periodic).

3. Cost Structure
- Exploration and drilling.
- Offshore logistics.
- Employee and operational expenses.

4. Competitive Strength
- Largest resource base in India.
- Strategic upstream monopoly-like position.
- Access to cheaper domestic crude for refiners.

5. Risks
- Brent price volatility affects profitability.
- Decline in mature fields.
- Government intervention in pricing.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Cyclical price patterns with high sensitivity to oil markets.
- High volumes during global crude shocks.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
ONGC mirrors:
- Upstream commodity price exposure,
- Earnings linked to global cycles.

8. Outlook
- Enhanced oil recovery,
- Deepwater exploration,
- Transition toward gas and renewables.
