Company: Infosys Ltd.
Industry: IT Services
ISIN: INE009A01021
Symbol: INFY

1. Business Overview
Infosys is one of India’s top IT service exporters providing consulting, digital transformation, cloud, AI, cybersecurity, and managed services to Fortune 500 clients globally.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Large digital transformation deals.
- Cloud modernization.
- Managed services (long-term annuity).
- Consulting and systems integration.

3. Cost Structure
- Employee salaries (dominant cost).
- Subcontracting + training.
- Onsite delivery and travel.
- Technology investments.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Strong brand in global IT outsourcing.
- High client retention.
- Ability to win mega-deals in US and Europe.
- Strong cash generation and dividend payout history.

5. Risks
- US recession slowing IT spending.
- Visa-related restrictions.
- Wage inflation affecting margins.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Moderate volatility, tracking global tech cycles.
- Volume spikes during earnings and guidance announcements.
- Sensitive to USD/INR movements.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
Infosys functions like a:
- Stable exporter with natural FX hedge,
- High-ROE service business,
- Low capex, high-FCF model.

Valuation driven by:
- EBIT margin,
- Deal pipeline,
- Digital revenue mix.

8. Outlook
- Stronger demand in cloud, AI, cybersecurity.
- Expansion in Europe and BFSI digital modernization.
