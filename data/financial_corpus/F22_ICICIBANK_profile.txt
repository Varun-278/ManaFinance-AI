Company: ICICI Bank Ltd.
Industry: Financial Services (Private Sector Bank)
ISIN: INE090A01021
Symbol: ICICIBANK

1. Business Overview
ICICI Bank is one of India's largest private-sector banks with a balanced retail–corporate loan book, strong digital banking capabilities, and subsidiaries in insurance, asset management, and securities.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Net interest income from retail and SME lending.
- Fee income (credit cards, payments, bancassurance).
- Treasury income.
- Subsidiary contributions.

3. Cost Structure
- Funding costs (CASA + borrowings).
- Credit provisioning.
- Technology and compliance costs.

4. Competitive Strengths
- One of the best NPA reduction stories in India.
- Digital-first banking infrastructure (iMobile, InstaBIZ).
- Strong capital position enabling growth.

5. Risks
- Credit costs rising in economic slowdowns.
- Increasing competition from fintechs.
- Corporate loan book cyclicality.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Liquid, stable, growing trend.
- High correlation with broader banking sector cycles.
- Volume boosts around quarterly earnings.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
ICICI Bank’s valuation hinges on:
- ROE expansion,
- Credit cost stability,
- NIM sustainability.

It reflects a maturing growth bank transitioning into a compounding franchise.

8. Outlook
- Retail loan growth,
- Payments and digital ecosystem expansion,
- Strong balance sheet guiding better valuations.
