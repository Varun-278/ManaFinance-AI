Company: Reliance Industries Ltd.
Industry: Energy / Petrochemicals / Retail / Telecom / Digital Services
ISIN: INE002A01018
Symbol: RELIANCE

1. Business Overview
Reliance is India’s largest conglomerate with leadership in refining, petrochemicals, retail, telecom (Jio), digital services, and new energy initiatives.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Petrochemical and refining spreads.
- Jio telecom subscribers and ARPU.
- Retail sales across groceries, electronics, fashion.
- Renewable energy (solar PV, green hydrogen) in future roadmap.

3. Cost Structure
- Crude oil procurement and refining fuel.
- Spectrum purchases and telecom capex.
- Retail expansion and logistics.
- R&D for new energy technologies.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Vertical integration in energy.
- Largest telecom subscriber base.
- Fast-growing retail footprint.
- Strong balance sheet and execution scale.

5. Risks
- Global crude volatility.
- Telecom price wars.
- Large capex cycles reducing FCF.

6. Dataset Behavior
- High liquidity, strong participation from institutions.
- Volatility increases around oil market events and Jio tariff news.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
Reliance is:
- A multi-segment conglomerate requiring SOTP valuation.
- Energy spreads + telecom ARPU + retail margins drive fair value.

8. Outlook
- Strong growth expected from retail + digital,
- Transition toward green energy leadership.
