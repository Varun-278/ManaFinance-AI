DERIVATIVES 

1. FUTURES CONTRACTS
Features:
• Exchange-traded
• Standardized
• Mark-to-market daily

Uses:
• Hedging
• Speculation
• Arbitrage (cash-futures)

Key Concepts:
• Basis = Spot – Futures
• Cost of carry model
• Rollovers

2. OPTIONS CONTRACTS
Call option → right to buy  
Put option → right to sell  

Greeks:
• Delta: sensitivity to price
• Gamma: sensitivity of delta
• Theta: time decay
• Vega: volatility sensitivity
• Rho: interest rate sensitivity

3. OPTION STRATEGIES
• Covered call
• Protective put
• Bull call spread
• Bear put spread
• Straddle
• Strangle
• Iron condor

4. VOLATILITY INDEX (INDIA VIX)
Measures expected volatility.
High VIX → risk-off environment.

5. RISKS
• Leverage risk
• Volatility spikes
• Liquidity risk

6. USES IN PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT
• Hedge downside
• Leverage directional bets
• Income generation
