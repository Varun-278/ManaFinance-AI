Company: Vedanta Ltd.
Industry: Metals (Diversified: Zinc, Aluminum, Oil & Gas)
ISIN: INE205A01025
Symbol: VEDL

1. Business Overview
Vedanta is a diversified natural resources company involved in zinc, aluminum, copper, power, and oil & gas.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Commodity prices (zinc, aluminum, oil).
- Volumes from mining and smelting.
- Power generation.

3. Cost Structure
- Fuel, power, alumina, and mining costs.
- High capex and debt servicing.

4. Competitive Strengths
- Integrated operations,
- Leadership in zinc via HZL,
- Large-scale mines.

5. Risks
- High leverage,
- Commodity volatility,
- Corporate governance concerns.

6. Dataset Behavior
- High volatility,
- Strong correlation with global commodity markets.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
VEDL is:
- A high-beta cyclical conglomerate,
- Valuation linked to commodity cycles and deleveraging.

8. Outlook
- Expansion in aluminum and zinc,
- Potential restructuring to unlock value.
