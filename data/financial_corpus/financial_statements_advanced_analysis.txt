ADVANCED FINANCIAL STATEMENT ANALYSIS 

1. FINANCIAL STATEMENTS
• Balance Sheet
• Income Statement
• Cash Flow Statement
• Notes to accounts (critical)

2. PROFITABILITY RATIOS
• ROE = Net Income / Shareholder Equity
• ROCE = EBIT / (Equity + Debt - Cash)
• NIM (banks)  
• EBITDA margin  
• PAT margin  

3. LEVERAGE & SOLVENCY
• Debt-to-equity
• Interest coverage ratio
• Net debt / EBITDA
Higher leverage → higher risk.

4. OPERATING METRICS
Manufacturing:
• Inventory turnover
• Operating cycle
• Asset turnover

Banks:
• GNPA / NNPA
• Provision coverage ratio (PCR)
• CASA ratio
• Credit-to-deposit ratio

5. CASH FLOW ANALYSIS
Operating > Investing + Financing → sustainable business.

Watch for:
• Aggressive revenue recognition
• Working capital buildup
• Negative OCF despite profits

6. WARNING SIGNS
• Sudden drop in cash position
• Rising debt without revenue growth
• Receivables growing faster than sales
• Qualified auditor reports
• Promoter pledging (India-specific)

7. EARNINGS QUALITY
High-quality earnings:
• Supported by cash
• Stable margins
• Conservative accounting
