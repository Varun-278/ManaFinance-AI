Company: Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd.
Industry: Automobile (SUVs, Tractors)
ISIN: INE101A01026
Symbol: M&M

1. Business Overview
M&M is a leader in utility vehicles and the world’s largest tractor manufacturer by volumes. The company has businesses in farm equipment, passenger SUVs, EVs, and subsidiaries in IT & finance.

2. Revenue Drivers
- SUV sales (Scorpio, XUV series, Thar).
- Tractor and farm equipment.
- Commercial vehicles.
- Subsidiaries: Tech Mahindra, Mahindra Finance, etc.

3. Cost Structure
- Commodity inputs (steel, rubber).
- Manufacturing and logistics.
- R&D for EVs and farm automation.

4. Competitive Strengths
- Strong rural reach via tractor business.
- Successful SUV brand franchises.
- Expanding EV roadmap (BE series).

5. Risks
- Cyclicality in rural income and tractor demand.
- EV competition.
- Supply chain constraints (semiconductors).

6. Dataset Behavior
- High correlation with rural/agri cycles.
- Volume spikes during new SUV launch cycles.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
M&M combines:
- Cyclical auto characteristics,
- Stable tractor cash flows,
- Optionality via EV and subsidiaries.

8. Outlook
- Aggressive EV rollout in SUVs,
- Strong farm mechanization demand.
