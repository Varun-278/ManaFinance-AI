FINANCIAL STATEMENTS 

1. THREE CORE STATEMENTS
a) Balance Sheet  
b) Income Statement  
c) Cash Flow Statement  

Together they give a comprehensive view of:
- Profitability
- Liquidity
- Leverage
- Operational efficiency
- Financial strength

2. LINKAGE BETWEEN STATEMENTS
Net income → retained earnings in equity  
Depreciation → added back in cash flow  
Capex → reduces cash, increases PPE  
Debt issued → increases cash and liabilities

3. BALANCE SHEET SNAPSHOT
Shows financial position at a point in time.

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

4. INCOME STATEMENT SNAPSHOT
Shows financial performance over a period:
Revenue → COGS → Gross Profit → OPEX → EBIT → PBT → PAT

5. CASH FLOW STATEMENT SNAPSHOT
Operating Cash Flow  
Investing Cash Flow  
Financing Cash Flow  

6. KEY ANALYTICAL RATIOS
Liquidity:
- Current ratio
- Quick ratio

Profitability:
- ROE, ROA
- Gross margin
- Net margin

Leverage:
- Debt/Equity  
- Interest coverage  

Efficiency:
- Working capital turnover  
- Inventory days  
- Receivable days
