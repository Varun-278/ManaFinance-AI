FOREX MARKETS & RUPEE DYNAMICS 

1. FACTORS INFLUENCING USD/INR
• Interest rate differentials (India vs US)
• Capital flows (FPI, FDI)
• Crude oil prices (India = net importer)
• RBI intervention
• Inflation differentials
• Global risk sentiment

2. TYPES OF EXCHANGE RATE REGIMES
India follows a managed float.
RBI intervenes to stabilize volatility, not to fix level.

3. BALANCE OF PAYMENTS (BoP)
Components:
• Current Account (trade balance, services, transfers)
• Capital Account (FDI, FPI)
• Financial Account (debt, equity flows)

4. IMPACT OF RUPEE MOVEMENT
Rupee Depreciation:
• Boosts exports
• Raises import bill (oil-heavy)
• Raises inflation

Rupee Appreciation:
• Reduces import costs
• Hurts IT/pharma export competitiveness

5. RBI INTERVENTION TOOLS
• FX swaps
• Spot purchases/sales
• Forward market operations

6. FOREX RESERVES
Built to protect against external shocks.
