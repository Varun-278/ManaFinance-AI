Company: Indian Oil Corporation Ltd.
Industry: Energy (Oil Refining & Marketing)
ISIN: INE242A01010
Symbol: IOC

1. Business Overview
Indian Oil Corporation (IOC) is India’s largest refining and fuel marketing company. It operates refineries, a vast network of fuel retail outlets, LPG distribution, aviation fuel supply, and petrochemical units.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Sale of petrol, diesel, LPG, ATF, and other fuels.
- Refining margins (GRMs) across multiple refineries.
- Petrochemical and lube sales.
- Aviation and bulk industrial fuel contracts.

3. Cost Structure
- Crude oil import cost.
- Refinery operating and maintenance expenses.
- Transportation and pipeline logistics.
- Retail network and marketing costs.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Largest fuel retail network in India.
- Integrated refining–marketing–pipeline system.
- Strong brand trust and LPG penetration.

5. Risks
- Government intervention in fuel pricing.
- Crude oil price volatility affecting margins.
- Energy transition toward EVs and cleaner energy.

6. Dataset-Based Behavior
- Cyclical price action linked to crude price cycles.
- Volume spikes around government policy decisions and OMC margin news.
- Defensive elements due to PSU backing and dividends.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
IOC exhibits:
- Commodity exposure via refining,
- Regulated/controlled pricing in retail,
- High operating leverage.

Valuation driven by:
- GRMs,
- Policy environment,
- Dividend yields.

8. Outlook
- Expansion into petrochemicals and gas.
- Investments in EV charging, alternative fuels (biofuels).
