EXCHANGE-TRADED FUNDS (ETFs) IN INDIA 

1. CHARACTERISTICS
ETFs combine features of:
- Mutual funds (diversification)
- Stocks (real-time trading)

2. TYPES OF ETFs
- Equity ETFs (NIFTY50, BANKNIFTY)
- Debt ETFs (short-term bonds, PSU bonds)
- Gold ETFs
- International ETFs
- Thematic ETFs

3. CREATION/REDEMPTION MECHANISM
Authorized Participants (APs) create units by delivering baskets of securities.

4. ADVANTAGES
- Lower expense ratios
- Transparency
- Liquidity
- Intraday price discovery

5. TRACKING ERROR
Difference between ETF returns and its index.
Caused by:
- Cash drag
- Rebalancing costs
- Liquidity issues

6. RISKS
- Market risk  
- Tracking risk  
- Liquidity mismatch
