Company: Maruti Suzuki India Ltd.
Industry: Automobile (Passenger Vehicles)
ISIN: INE585B01010
Symbol: MARUTI

1. Business Overview
Maruti is India's largest passenger vehicle manufacturer with dominance in entry-level and compact categories. Backed by Suzuki Motor Corp., the company has an expansive dealer/service network and strong brand recall.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Small car, hatchback, and compact SUV sales.
- After-sales services and genuine parts.
- Exports of compact cars to emerging markets.
- New hybrid and CNG portfolio expansion.

3. Cost Structure
- Steel, aluminum, and commodity inputs.
- Vendor ecosystem and logistics.
- Employee and plant operations.
- R&D for new platforms and hybrid vehicles.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Unmatched dealer network and after-sales ecosystem.
- Strong cost efficiency and scale.
- Market dominance in sub-₹10 lakh segment.

5. Risks
- Rising EV adoption pressuring ICE volumes.
- Competition from Hyundai, Tata Motors, Kia.
- Commodity price volatility impacting margins.

6. Dataset Insights
- Moderate volatility with periodic volume spikes around launches.
- Uptrends often follow industry-wide sales momentum.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
Maruti exhibits:
- High operating leverage,
- Low debt,
- Valuation sensitivity to market share.

8. Outlook
- Expansion into hybrid SUVs,
- Future EV platform collaborations with Suzuki.
