INCOME STATEMENT ANALYSIS 

1. PURPOSE
The income statement provides insight into profitability, cost structure, revenue drivers, and
operational performance.

2. STRUCTURE
Revenue  
– COGS  
= Gross Profit  

– Operating expenses  
= EBIT  

– Interest + Other expenses  
= PBT  

– Taxes  
= PAT  

3. KEY PROFITABILITY RATIOS
Gross Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue  
EBIT Margin = EBIT / Revenue  
Net Margin = PAT / Revenue  

ROE = Net Income / Equity  
ROCE = EBIT / (Equity + Debt – Cash)  

4. REVENUE QUALITY ANALYSIS
- Organic vs inorganic growth
- Scaling efficiency
- Recurring vs cyclical revenues
- Geographical diversification

5. COST STRUCTURE INSIGHTS
- Operating leverage
- Variable vs fixed costs
- SG&A efficiency

6. EARNINGS QUALITY
High-quality earnings come from:
- Core operations  
- Strong cash conversion  

Low-quality earnings arise from:
- Accounting adjustments  
- One-time gains  

7. COMMON RED FLAGS
- Declining margins  
- Large other income dependencies  
- One-off adjustments masking trends
