Company: Larsen & Toubro Ltd. (L&T)
Industry: Construction / Infrastructure / Engineering
ISIN: INE018A01030
Symbol: LT

1. Business Overview
L&T is India’s largest engineering and construction conglomerate with presence in EPC projects (infrastructure, power, hydrocarbons), heavy engineering, defense, IT services (via subsidiaries), and financial services.

2. Revenue Drivers
- Order inflows in infrastructure and hydrocarbon EPC.
- Execution of large domestic and international projects.
- Subsidiary contributions (IT, technology, finance).

3. Cost Structure
- Project materials and subcontracting.
- Employee and site execution costs.
- Working capital and finance costs for large EPC orders.

4. Competitive Advantages
- Strong track record in complex EPC projects.
- Diversified order book across sectors and geographies.
- High engineering capabilities and pre-qualification credentials.

5. Risks
- Project delays and cost overruns.
- Working capital lock-up impacting cash flows.
- Commodity and labor inflation pressures.

6. Dataset-Based Behavior
- Cyclical but structurally upward with infra cycles.
- Volume spikes when large orders are announced.
- High beta to Indian capex and investment cycle.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
L&T is:
- A capex cycle proxy for the Indian economy,
- Valuation linked to order book visibility and execution margins.

Key valuation metrics:
- Order inflow growth,
- Core EPC EBIT margins,
- Reduction of non-core exposures.

8. Outlook
- Beneficiary of government infra push,
- Divestment of non-core segments to improve ROE.
