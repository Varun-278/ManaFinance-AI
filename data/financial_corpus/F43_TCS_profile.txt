Company: Tata Consultancy Services Ltd.
Industry: IT Services

Symbol: TCS

1. Business Overview
TCS is India’s largest IT services company offering BFSI, retail, healthcare, cloud, AI/analytics, and consulting services globally.

2. Revenue Drivers
- BFSI and retail clients.
- Cloud transformation and managed services.
- Large multi-year outsourcing deals.

3. Cost Structure
- Employee wages (major cost component).
- Training, bench cost.
- Delivery center operations.

4. Competitive Strengths
- Strong brand and diversified client base.
- High client retention.
- Industry-leading operating margins.

5. Risks
- Wage inflation.
- Slowdowns in US and Europe tech spending.
- Automation reducing labor-arbitrage benefits.

6. Dataset Behavior
- Low volatility; stable compounding stock.
- Moves mostly around results/guidance.

7. Financial Theory Interpretation
Behaves like:
- A high-ROE compounder,
- Cash-rich, low-beta,
- Valuation tied to earnings growth predictability.

8. Outlook
- Strong demand for AI/automation services.
