MUTUAL FUNDS IN INDIA 

1. TYPES OF MUTUAL FUNDS
a) Equity funds
b) Debt funds
c) Hybrid funds
d) Sector/thematic funds
e) Index funds
f) Liquid/overnight funds

2. FUND STRUCTURE
Sponsors → Trustees → AMC → Fund Manager → Custodian

3. NAV DETERMINATION
NAV = (Market value of securities – Liabilities) / Units outstanding  
NAV changes daily based on market prices and fund performance.

4. SEBI CATEGORIES
35 distinct categories under equity, debt, and hybrid.

5. PERFORMANCE METRICS
- CAGR  
- Sharpe ratio  
- Alpha  
- Beta  
- Sortino ratio  
- Expense ratio  

6. RISKS
- Market risk  
- Interest rate risk  
- Credit risk  
- Liquidity risk  

7. TAXATION
Equity:
- STCG: 15%
- LTCG: 10% above ₹1 lakh

Debt:
- Taxed as per slab rate (post 2023 changes)

8. INDUSTRY TRENDS
- Rapid rise of SIPs  
- Passive investing growth  
- Investor education initiatives
