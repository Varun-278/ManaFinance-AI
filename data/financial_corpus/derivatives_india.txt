DERIVATIVES MARKET IN INDIA 

1. PURPOSE OF DERIVATIVES
Derivatives help:
- Hedge risk
- Speculate
- Arbitrage
- Improve market efficiency

2. TYPES OF DERIVATIVES
a) Futures  
b) Options  
c) Forwards  
d) Swaps (OTC)  

3. EQUITY DERIVATIVES
NSE is the world’s largest derivatives exchange by volume.

Products:
- Index futures & options (NIFTY, BANKNIFTY)
- Stock futures & options (F&O list)

4. OPTION GREEKS
Delta — sensitivity to price  
Gamma — sensitivity to delta  
Theta — time decay  
Vega — sensitivity to volatility  
Rho — sensitivity to interest rates  

5. IMPLIED VOLATILITY
High IV = expensive options  
Low IV = cheaper options  
India VIX measures market volatility expectations.

6. STRATEGIES
- Covered call  
- Protective put  
- Bull call spread  
- Iron condor  
- Calendar spreads  

7. RISKS
- Leverage risk  
- Liquidity risk  
- Gap risk  
- Volatility crush
